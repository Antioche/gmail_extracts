When there is a new notification it doesn't mean the result of the same date or recent date-these notifications are automated and even if an old result/note has been updated recently from your health centre-it triggers a notification. It is possible an old record was recently filed by your practice.

If you click on "Health Records" in the My health section it will take you to the page where all the records are kept. From your email, it states that you have a new notification in the lab results notification. So you can check under Lab Results tab. Note any section with an underline means there are new unread records and Unread records will have a green background. See a below screenshot of a test patient I have shared.
