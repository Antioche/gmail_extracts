Anti-bug balm

84g olive oil

16g yellow beeswax

5g citronella essential oil

2g Himalayan cedarwood essential oil

2g lemongrass essential oil

1g rosemary essential oil

1g geranium essential oil

Melt the olive oil and beeswax gently in a pot. Take off the heat.

Add the essential oils and stir.

Pour into a 100ml jar and leave to set.
