Service and dietary requirement information:

Unfortunately Brasserie Le Cordon is now unable to make any menu alterations, or cater to any specific dietary requirements (including religious diets) or food allergies.  We are a training restaurant and our students must be able to produce dishes using a wide range of ingredients, and for this reason our menus are set. We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause. Examples of previous menus can be found at: https://www.cordonbleu.edu/new-zealand/events/en. We are a fully licensed restaurant so we do not offer BYO.

Brasserie Le Cordon Bleu has a maximum table size of 12 to ensure quality of service and manageability for all our training students.

All dining experiences at Le Cordon Bleu must be pre-booked and we cannot accept any customers without a reservation.

Please allow time to sit, relax and enjoy this unique dining experience. We give a guide of one hour for our high tea and three course services, one and a half hours for our five course option, two and half hours for our seven course and three hours for our ten course degustation menus.
